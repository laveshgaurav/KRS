"History Of KRS"

The renowned 'KIIT Robotics Society' was founded by a student of School Of Electrical Engineering named 'Nitin Krishna' in 2011. After which he joined KRS as Jr. Scientist. In 2015 he left KSAC and joined PNB at J&K as a banker.
He was accompained by 'Diptish Saha' who was co-founder of KRS belonged to School Of Electrical Engineering. He completed is masters from KIIT and became a lecturer at KIIT polytechnic. Currently he's currently pursuing PhD program at University Of Dallas.
